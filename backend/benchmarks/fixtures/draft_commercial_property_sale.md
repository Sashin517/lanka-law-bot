# DRAFT AGREEMENT FOR SALE OF COMMERCIAL PROPERTY

**Date**: 15th January 2026

**Between**:

1. **Mr. Aravinda Perera** of No. 45, Galle Road, Colombo 03 (hereinafter referred to as "the Seller")

AND

2. **Lanka Trading (Pvt) Ltd**, a company incorporated under the laws of Sri Lanka, having its registered office at No. 112, Duplication Road, Colombo 04 (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer")

---

## 1. Property Description

The Seller agrees to sell, and the Buyer agrees to purchase, the commercial property described as follows:

- **Address**: No. 78, Main Street, Colombo 11
- **Extent**: Approximately 15 Perches
- **Deed Reference**: Deed No. 4521 attested by K.M. Fonseka, Notary Public, dated 10th March 2015
- **Local Authority**: Colombo Municipal Council
- **Current Use**: Commercial warehouse and office space

## 2. Purchase Price

The total purchase price for the said property shall be **Sri Lankan Rupees Seventy-Five Million (LKR 75,000,000/-)**, payable as follows:

- (a) An initial deposit of LKR 10,000,000/- upon execution of this agreement.
- (b) The balance sum of LKR 65,000,000/- to be paid within ninety (90) days from the date of this agreement, upon transfer of clear title.

## 3. Title and Encumbrances

The Seller warrants that:

- (a) The Seller is the absolute owner of the said property and has good and marketable title thereto.
- (b) The property is free from all encumbrances, liens, mortgages, caveats, and claims of whatsoever nature.
- (c) All rates, taxes, and assessments due to the local authority have been paid up to date.

## 4. Conditions Precedent

This agreement is conditional upon:

- (a) The Buyer obtaining satisfactory results from a title search conducted at the Land Registry.
- (b) The Seller providing a survey plan prepared by a licensed surveyor.
- (c) Obtaining all necessary approvals from the relevant local authority for the transfer.

## 5. Possession

Vacant possession of the property shall be delivered to the Buyer upon receipt of the full purchase price and execution of the deed of transfer.

## 6. Default

In the event of default by the Buyer, the Seller shall be entitled to forfeit the deposit paid. In the event of default by the Seller, the Seller shall refund the deposit together with interest at the rate of 12% per annum.

## 7. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.

## 8. Entire Agreement

This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements, whether written or oral.
