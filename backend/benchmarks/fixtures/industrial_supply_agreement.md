# INDUSTRIAL MACHINERY SUPPLY AGREEMENT

**Agreement Reference**: IMSA-2026-0015
**Date**: 20th March 2026

**Between**:

1. **HeavyTech Engineering (Pvt) Ltd**, No. 55, Industrial Zone, Katunayake (hereinafter "the Seller")

AND

2. **CeylonBuild Construction (Pvt) Ltd**, No. 88, Kandy Road, Kadawatha (hereinafter "the Buyer")

---

## 1. Goods

The Seller agrees to supply the following industrial machinery ("the Goods"):

| Item | Description | Qty | Unit Price (LKR) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hydraulic Excavator Model HT-350X | 2 | 18,500,000 |
| 2 | Concrete Batching Plant CBP-120 | 1 | 32,000,000 |
| 3 | Tower Crane TC-65D | 1 | 45,000,000 |

**Total Contract Value**: LKR 114,000,000/-

## 2. Delivery

- (a) Delivery shall be made to the Buyer's site at Kadawatha within ninety (90) days of order confirmation.
- (b) Risk of loss shall pass to the Buyer upon delivery.

## 3. Payment Terms

- (a) 30% advance payment upon execution of this agreement.
- (b) 50% upon shipment confirmation from the manufacturer.
- (c) 20% balance upon delivery and physical inspection.

## 4. Condition of Goods — "As-Is" Basis

**4.1** THE GOODS ARE SOLD STRICTLY ON AN "AS-IS, WHERE-IS" BASIS.

**4.2** The Buyer acknowledges and agrees that it has independently inspected and evaluated the Goods and is satisfied with their condition, quality, and fitness for the Buyer's intended purposes.

## 5. Waiver of Warranties

**5.1** THE SELLER EXPRESSLY DISCLAIMS AND THE BUYER EXPRESSLY WAIVES ALL WARRANTIES, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED, OR STATUTORY, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO:

- (a) Any warranty that the Goods correspond with their description;
- (b) Any warranty or condition of merchantable quality;
- (c) Any warranty or condition that the Goods are reasonably fit for any particular purpose, whether or not such purpose has been communicated to the Seller;
- (d) Any warranty arising from course of dealing or usage of trade.

**5.2** The Buyer agrees that the Seller's sole obligation is to deliver the Goods as described in Section 1 above, and no further obligations, whether statutory or otherwise, shall attach to the Seller.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

The Seller's maximum aggregate liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total contract value stated in Section 1.

## 7. Inspection and Acceptance

The Buyer shall inspect the Goods within seven (7) days of delivery. Failure to provide written notice of defects within this period shall constitute unconditional acceptance.

## 8. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of Sri Lanka.
