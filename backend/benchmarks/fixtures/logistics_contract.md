**Effective Date**: 1st February 2026

**Between**:

1. **SwiftCargo Logistics (Pvt) Ltd**, No. 200, Baseline Road, Colombo 09 (hereinafter "the Service Provider")

AND

2. **The Client** (details as per the attached Schedule A)

---

## 1. Scope of Services

The Service Provider agrees to provide the following logistics services:

- (a) Warehousing and storage of goods
- (b) Domestic transportation and distribution
- (c) Freight forwarding (sea and air)
- (d) Customs clearance and documentation
- (e) Inventory management and reporting

## 2. Service Standards

The Service Provider shall exercise reasonable care and diligence in the handling, storage, and transportation of the Client's goods in accordance with industry standards.

## 3. Insurance

The Client is solely responsible for obtaining adequate insurance coverage for all goods entrusted to the Service Provider. The Service Provider does not provide any insurance for the Client's goods.

## 4. Fees and Payment

- (a) Service fees shall be as set out in Schedule B attached hereto.
- (b) Invoices are payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.
- (c) Late payments shall attract interest at 15% per annum.

## 5. Term and Termination

- (a) This contract shall be for an initial term of twelve (12) months.
- (b) Either party may terminate this contract by giving sixty (60) days written notice.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

**6.1** The Service Provider shall not be liable for any loss, damage, delay, or expense of whatsoever nature arising from the provision of services under this contract, including but not limited to:

- (a) **Death or personal injury** caused by the negligence, acts, or omissions of the Service Provider, its employees, agents, or subcontractors;
- (b) **Loss of or damage to property** (including the Client's goods) caused by the negligence of the Service Provider;
- (c) Any indirect, consequential, or special damages, including loss of profits, business interruption, or loss of goodwill.

**6.2** In any event, the total aggregate liability of the Service Provider under this contract shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

**6.3** The Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Service Provider from all claims, damages, and expenses arising from any third-party claims related to the goods.

## 7. Force Majeure

The Service Provider shall not be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations where such failure or delay results from circumstances beyond its reasonable control.

## 8. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This contract shall be governed by the laws of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. Any disputes shall be referred to arbitration in Colombo.

## 9. Entire Agreement

This contract, together with its Schedules, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
